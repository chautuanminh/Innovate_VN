Frequently Asked Questions

What stage of startups do you fund? +

We fund companies across all early stages. Some startups apply with just an idea, while others already have revenue and product-market fit.

Do I need a co-founder to apply? +

No, solo founders are welcome to apply. However, having a balanced team generally increases your chances of success.

How does the SAFE funding work? +

We invest $150K on a standard SAFE with a Most Favored Nation (MFN) clause. It is fast, standard, and founder-friendly.

Do I need to be based in Vietnam? +

Your target market or core product team should have a strong connection to Southeast Asia, but we accept remote teams globally.

Can I apply multiple times? +

Yes, many startups are accepted on their second or third application. We highly value progress between applications.

How long does the accelerator program last? +

The core program runs for 3 months, culminating in a Demo Day. However, Innovate Vietnam support continues for the lifetime of your company.

What if my company is already incorporated abroad? +

We regularly work with Singapore entities or Cayman structures. We have a robust legal team to help structure the investment appropriately.

Do you take board seats? +

No, we do not take board seats. We are advisors and partners, but you run your company.

What happens if my startup fails? +

Startups fail. If you act ethically and do your best, there is no penalty or personal liability. In fact, many founders start new companies and we fund them again.

How do I prepare for the interview? +

Be concise, know your numbers, and have a clear understanding of your unique insight into the market problem.
