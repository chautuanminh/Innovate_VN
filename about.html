We Help Startups Succeed

Innovate Vietnam provides seed funding, world-class mentorship, and an unparalleled network for Southeast Asia's most ambitious founders. We exist to accelerate the next generation of category-defining tech companies in Vietnam.

Our Story

2019

Innovate Vietnam founded to bridge the gap between global capital and local talent.

2021

Launched the IV Accelerator program. First batch of 15 startups raises $50M+ combined.

2023

Portfolio valuation crosses $1 Billion. First two unicorns minted (Fintech & Logistics).

2025

Expanded to a broader Southeast Asian focus. Opened the new Innovation Campus in HCMC.
